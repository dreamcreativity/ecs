Casa Loma

Casa Loma - Completed in 1914, the historic Casa Loma is a beautiful building and grounds that will take you back in time. Carefully restored and maintained, this building shows visitors what life in 1925 was like. Visit the elaborately decorated rooms, look at the library, examine the suites, and then take the tunnel out to the stables where horses were kept and explore the gardens.
