Toronto Summer Kids Program

Overview

This program is dedicated to children around the world who are from 9 to 13 years old age only and are looking for a high quality English summer camp in the city of Toronto. The program is a combination of English lessons, excursions and activities for them. It is hosted at the University of Toronto downtown campus where everything happens for them; English classes, residence, cafeteria and a lot more. The program is offered in two options. Residential and Day Program/Self-arranged. The residential program is for those students who live in the residence and take the full program, while the Day Program/Self-arranged one is dedicated to students who participate in the program from Monday to Friday from 09:00 – 18:00. Usually the students in this program live with family or relatives in the city of Toronto.

The residential life and campus

The program is hosted at the University of Toronto’s downtown campus at the heart of the city, close to Bay and Museum subway stations and walking distance to great local attractions. The residence hosting the students offers a wide array of amenities.

Single and double rooms are available for the students. Each room offers bed, drawer, desk, chair, dresser and internet access. Linen are changed every 7 days for the students as well as general maintenance of the room. Washrooms and showers are shared with other students.

The cafeteria is a 2 minute walk from the residence, where students are offered an all-you-can-eat, buffet style options daily.

Click here to see pictures of the campus and campus fact sheet

Academics and English classes

Students take 20 English classes per week, Monday to Friday in the mornings and they are also hosted inside the campus. The classrooms are within 5 minute walk from the residence.

Activities and social program

At ESC we are proud to offer full activity calendar to our students. 7 days a week, every afternoon and every evening we have an event, activity or excursion for our students. Activities and events for our kids take into consideration their age and are built to enhance not only their English skills but above all their whole experience in Toronto with us. All tickets and all expenses of the activities and events that we organize and already paid for and children only have to enjoy them.

Kingston Summer Teen Program

Overview

The program will be a combination of academic learning, workshops, visits to attractions and cultural learning tours. The program is built with teenagers in mind and taking into account what they want for their future. It is going to be an all-inclusive program and it will run in July and August 2016

The residential life and campus

An important component of the life of a student in a summer camp will be the living on campus. St Lawrence College offers great facilities that students can benefit from. En-suite bathrooms, air-conditioned rooms, gym, outdoor fields and a lot more are available for students. Students are supervised 24/7 from our staff and we will always be there for them when needed.

Girls and boys live in different areas of the residence with our staff living together with them in the same area. Whether they are learning in the classroom or outdoors our staff is going to always be there. Our staff is experienced and trained to work with teenagers from different cultures and we also speak your language if it is necessary.

Click here to see pictures of the campus

Academics and English classes

The Academic component will be focused on achieving the following results:

Language acquisition
ESC is a leader in English teaching for young students. We have successfully run programs for more than 10 years. This will be done through classes that are focused on language learning components specifically such as grammar, conversation, reading and writing

Understanding of education system in Canada
Some of the classes will be focused exclusively on teaching the students how education system works in Canada, what to expect when learning in a post-secondary institution, learning tips and what it offers to international students

Specific knowledge of different subjects
Students through different workshops and invitation to different classes will be exposed to a few departments of higher education and experience it hands on the benefits of studying in a post-secondary institution in Canada, how classes are run and the technologies used

Other information on Canada and its opportunities
Through these classes and workshops students will learn about all opportunities that exist in Canada as well as how educational, cultural and learning institutions work. For example visits to public libraries, workshop on living in Canada and other

Activities and social program

This component of the program is also vital for the students as it ensures that they are actually experiencing the theory with their own eyes. Visiting educational institutions, museums and famous world renowned landmarks will expose them to what Canada has to offer. Niagara Falls, Toronto, Montreal, Ottawa, Camping, Hiking, Museum visits etc. are only some of the amazing places and things that students will get to see and do

All tickets and all expenses of the activities and events that we organize and already paid for and children only have to enjoy them.

Family Programs

Our family programs are an amazing opportunity for families around the world to spend some great time in the city of Toronto during the summer and have an amazing vacation. The program runs at the same time as the summer camps for children and teenagers. We offer families options to be hosted in other host families or in apartments. During the week children participate in our Day Program/Self-Arranged option, while parents take classes at our adult center with other adults.

Schedule for parents

08:55 Drop child at junior camp location
10:00 – 13:00 English classes
18:00 Pick up your child

Schedule for children
08:55 Arrive at junior camp location
09:00 – 18:00 Junior program
18:00 Meet your parent

The schedule is from Monday to Friday. Evenings and weekends are for families to enjoy together and visit what this city has to offer. As a bonus we offer families a free trip to Niagara Falls for the entire family!

Accommodations

We offer families two main options

1. Host families. This option comes with two meals a day (breakfast and dinner). Host families are located around the city and are on average between 30 – 40 minutes walk away from the schools

2. Apartments. We work with TownInn and offer families amazing option in an apartment style. This option has no meals included but offers families full kitchen. The apartments are walking distance from the junior camp location and about 15 minutes commute from the adult center.

Toronto Year Round Programs

Overview

The programs throughout the year are intended for students or groups that are willing to come to Toronto and experience a great vacation study program. The programs are most of the time customized and are built as per group wish.

The programs are run at any time of the year from September to May. You can choose your time of travel, the number and type of lessons you want (or may be no lessons at all), accommodation options and other services including a social program with activities and excursions that better suits you

You can build a program by choosing :

Trip duration
Number of lessons (or not)
Accommodation options (host family or any other available option)
Meal options
Number of activities and excursions
Airport pick up and drop off
Transportation options

During the year you also have the option of experiencing Canadian private high schools and immersing into the daily learning with other students. All you have to do is what you want and we will make it happen
