Toronto City

Toronto’s past and present make it a great place to visit and to live: a clean, safe, modern city that is easy to get around an offers lots to do and see. Toronto is now North America’s fourth-largest city and its multi-ethnic population representing around 200 nations. Newcomers inevitably add something to the city. Fresh ideas of community are incorporated into existing ones so Toronto or T.O. (as it is also known) is always in flux. New residents, students, travelers, and curious locals are constantly reinventing the city that they want.
