Junior Programs

For students from 9 to 17 years of age inclusive

Toronto Summer Kids Program

This program is dedicated to children around the world who are from 9 to 13 years old age only and are looking for a high quality English summer camp in the city of Toronto.

Toronto Summer Teen Program

This program is dedicated to teenagers around the world who are from 14 to 17 years old age only and are looking for a high quality English summer camp in the city of Toronto.

Kingston Summer Teen Program

The summer camp is an amazing opportunity for students from 14 to 17 years old to get to know Canada, its education system and learn useful knowledge.

Family Programs

Our family programs are an amazing opportunity for families around the world to spend some great time in the city of Toronto during the summer and have an amazing vacation.

Toronto Year Round Programs

The programs throughout the year are intended for students or groups that are willing to come to Toronto and experience a great vacation study program. The programs are most of the time customized and are built as per group wish.
